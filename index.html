About

3D Artist
& Specialist.

I'm Pablo del Amo, a 3D Artist with over five years of experience producing game-ready assets. My focus is weapons, props, hard surface and environment work — the kind of work where technical precision and visual quality have to coexist.

My workflow spans the full production pipeline: high-poly modeling, retopology, UV optimization, baking, and PBR texturing. I'm experienced with both stylized and realistic pipelines, and I regularly validate assets in Unreal Engine to ensure they perform correctly before delivery.

I work remotely with international teams and I'm available for freelance contracts, outsourcing work, and full-time positions. Open to relocation for the right project.
